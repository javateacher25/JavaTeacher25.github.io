## Object-Oriented Programming (Java)

### Core OOP Topics
- Classes & objects
- Encapsulation
- Inheritance
- Polymorphism
- Abstraction
- Association, Aggregation & Composition

### SOLID Principles (inside OOP)
- **S** – Single Responsibility (SRP)
- **O** – Open/Closed (OCP)
- **L** – Liskov Substitution (LSP)
- **I** – Interface Segregation (ISP)
- **D** – Dependency Inversion (DIP)

Why SOLID matters:
- Easier maintenance
- Better testing
- Cleaner architecture
- Foundation for Dependency Injection (Jakarta EE / Spring)
